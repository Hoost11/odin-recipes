Quatro Formaggi Pizza Recipe

Description

Just the best pizza there can be!

A mouthful of the tastiest cheese you can gather in one place

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Gorgonzola
  • Parmigiano Reggiano
  • Cheddar

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Assemble the pizza
  3. Put the cheese on top
  4. Bake until ready
